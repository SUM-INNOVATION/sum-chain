Tokenomics

800 billion. Fixed forever.

Koppa (Ϙ) is sized for everyday payments by eight billion people: whole-number prices, a flat fee of 0.001 Ϙ, and a supply that never inflates.

Supply

Every Koppa that will ever exist was minted at genesis: 800 billion Ϙ, fixed forever. There is no mining, no block reward, and no new issuance. With roughly 100 Ϙ per person on Earth, everyday amounts stay in whole numbers: a coffee is 5 Ϙ, not 0.00005 of something.

Fees

The minimum fee is 0.001 Ϙ flat, never a percentage. Sending 5 Ϙ and sending 5 million Ϙ cost the same. All fees in a block go to the validator that proposed it; nothing is burned. Validator revenue therefore scales with real network usage instead of inflation.

Capacity and velocity

The chain sustains over 1,000 transactions per second, about 86 million transactions per day, with fees that stay negligible at scale. Koppa is designed for velocity: money that moves like cash rather than sitting like a collectible. The only deflationary pressure is coins lost over time.
